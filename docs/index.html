Welcome to Rigwars, the cryptomining Idle game!


How to play:

- Install Metamask or Trust Wallet

- Have an account with some amount of Ether
- Press the Start Game button!
Contract

Game guide:

- RigWars designed to be an almost infinite Idle game, which will need your action every day to maintain your Rigs just like Cryptomining in real life.
Your Dashboard:
Game components:
  • Rig Parts:
    - You can buy 8th different Mining solution from Cloud Mining -> Quantum Computer, to unlock the next level you should pay extra price.
  • Prestige level:
    - You can Upgrade your prestige which will give you permanent Hashrate increase, but it will reset your balance to 0 and half the numbers of your Rigs.
    Keep in mind that you can't reach some part of the game without upgrading your Prestige!
  • Boosters:
    - There are rare items in the game which can be invested by Eth, these are giving you permanent bonuses while you are owner of them. You can be outbuyed any time then you will get back your investment!
  • ICO:
    - Every day a new ICO start, with investing your Tokens into ICO allow you to get share from the Contract HODL!
  • Rig Token:
    - Your RigWars balance are fully implemented ERC-20 Tokens, 1 RigToken worth 100.000.000 RigCoin you can Trade RigToken on Forkdelta or other Exchanges.
  • Referral system:
    - Ref your friend or promote Rigwars without any limitation you will get 50% of their Prestige level up cost. You can find your referral link in the footer.
